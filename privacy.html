Privacy Policy — FixSEO

Last updated: July 2026

1. What Data We Collect

When you use FixSEO, we collect:

  • Your website URL
  • Your email address

This is collected directly from you via the audit request form.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Run the SEO audit on your website
  • Send you the audit report
  • Contact you about your audit if needed

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. We do not store audit results permanently unless you purchase the full report.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties, except:

  • Gumroad (payment processing for paid audits)
  • Brevo (email delivery)

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at vic@fixseo.uk.

6. Cookies

FixSEO does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: vic@fixseo.uk
